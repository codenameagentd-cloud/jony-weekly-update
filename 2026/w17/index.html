$ cat motion-decision.log
01

Strong motion was rejected

The early motion direction felt too much like “effects.” Large slide movement and obvious transitions reduced the sense of polish.

Rejected
02

Shared subtle baseline worked better

A quieter system built on short fades, line draws, type lift, and restrained pacing felt more expensive and more attached to layout.

Keep
03

Over-differentiated motion also failed

When each template received a fully separate motion language, the overall smoothness was lost. The better solution was one calm baseline plus one signature gesture per style.

Learned
04

Final decision for this week

Keep each template’s own visual personality, but preserve the smoother baseline motion. Terminal Ribbons Stream was chosen under that principle.

Chosen
